About UKMON

UK Meteor Observation Network (UKMON) is a free and open group of astonomy enthusiasts.

We collect scientifically important data from our cameras and this data is shared with other groups worldwide to expand our understanding of the Solar System.

There is no fee to join our group, no meetings and no nonsense. Meteor detection is our hobby and we all like to enjoy this wonderful activity.
