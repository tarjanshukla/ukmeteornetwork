Have you been lucky enough to see a fireball or a bright meteor?

Or maybe you are not quite sure what you saw? Fill in this Fireball Report Form and alert scientists worldwide.

Submit your report

How does it work?

It's easy and fun to report your fireball sighting in 12 steps. We encourage you to make the report form even if you are not 100% sure what you saw. It is important however, to be as descriptive as possible in your report.

If you have an image or a CCTV camera footage, we would like to have a look. Please get in touch with us!

Just follow simple and clear instructions on the report form and try to be as accurate as possible.

This form compiles very useful report for our citizen scientists to investigate.

We appreciate every report and all reports are stored, analysed and available to scientists all over the world.
